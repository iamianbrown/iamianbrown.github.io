education

Major: 
Engineering Physics

Physics is an effective way to make sense of complex and counterintuitive topics. By understanding the fundamentals, learning the things that build on top of them becomes much easier. I chose to study physics out of a desire to understand the fundamental truths of the universe. As I prepare to graduate, I am eager to start building on top of this knowledge.

The best analytical framework for understanding the future is physics.

- Elon Musk

Elective Option: Philosophy

Philosophy and physics are each built upon a tradition of analytical rigor. Physics evaluates mathematical statements about the world, while philosophy evaluates linguistic ones. By studying philosophy, I have strengthened my mind by reading difficult texts, sharpened my awareness by learning new concepts, and improved my communication by writing about them.

Project Team:
AIAA Liquid Rocket Initiative

Inspired by the SpaceX Starship development program, my friend Bart and I decided to start our own student rocketry group. One year in, we have secured over $15,000  in donations and are about to run our first hot fire test. This project provides a nice, concrete counterpoint to my other more abstract pursuits, and it gives me an opportunity to learn about the less academic aspects of engineering, such as leadership.
